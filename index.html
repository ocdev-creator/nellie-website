a gentle way to stay close

nellie is a simple tablet that helps families stay connected to loved ones through photos, messages and familiar memories

get started how it works
with nellie your loved one can...

view photos & messages

from family & friends, the moment they're sent

watch personal photo slideshows

treasured memories, gently rotating all day

listen to familiar music

the songs and stations they've always loved

see gentle reminders

of time, date & upcoming visits

why nellie?

cognitive decline and ageing can often lead to becoming less connected

over 8 million UK residents live alone or in care homes

modern technology becomes increasingly confusing

staying connected becomes harder

loneliness can build quietly, day by day

nellie was designed to make connection simple again
who is nellie for?

for families who want to stay connected

Whether your loved one lives:

  • in their own home
  • in assisted accommodation
  • or in a care home

nellie helps you stay present in their everyday life, through photos, messages, music and familiar memories.
