why nellie?

cognitive decline and ageing can often lead to becoming less connected

over 8 million UK residents live alone or in care homes

modern technology becomes increasingly confusing

staying connected becomes harder

loneliness can build quietly, day by day

nellie was designed to make connection simple again
who is nellie for?

for families who want to stay connected

Whether your loved one lives:

  • in their own home
  • in assisted accommodation
  • or in a care home

nellie helps you stay present in their everyday life, through photos, messages, music and familiar memories.
