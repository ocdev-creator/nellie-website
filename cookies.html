Cookies

Last updated: 17 June 2026

We keep cookies and similar storage to the minimum needed to run the site. We do not use tracking, analytics or advertising cookies, and we do not share anything with advertisers.

What we use, and why

Essential local storage

When you start a purchase, your browser stores a small amount of information so the checkout works. This includes a one-off reference (a "device token") and the details you enter, kept on your device so your account can be created after payment, for example if you return to the page. This stays in your browser, is not used to track you, and is not shared.

Payment (Stripe)

When you pay, our payment provider Stripe sets its own essential cookies to process the payment securely and help prevent fraud. You can read more in Stripe's cookie policy.

What we don't use

We currently set no analytics, performance or advertising cookies. If that changes in future, we will update this page and, where the law requires it, ask for your consent first.

Managing cookies

You can control or clear cookies and site data through your browser settings. Blocking essential items may stop the checkout from working properly, but you can browse the rest of the site normally.
