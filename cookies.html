Cookies

Last updated: 16 July 2026

We keep things simple and private. This site does not use tracking or advertising cookies, we never sell your information, and there is nothing you need to accept to browse it.

What we use, and why

Analytics (Plausible)

We use Plausible, a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to understand roughly how many people visit and which pages they read, so we can keep making the site gentler and easier. It is cookieless: it stores nothing on your device, collects no personal data and does not track you across other sites. Because of that there is no cookie banner and nothing to consent to.

When you register your interest

If you fill in the register-interest form, the details you enter are sent to us so we can get in touch at launch. That is handled by our host, Netlify, and is not used to track you or shared with advertisers.

Fonts

Our typeface is served by Google Fonts, so your browser requests the font files from Google when a page loads. This sets no cookies.

What we don't use

No advertising cookies, no cross-site tracking, no data brokers. We do not build a profile of you, and we never sell or share your information for marketing.

Managing cookies and site data

Because we don't set tracking cookies, there is nothing to switch off here. You can still control or clear cookies and site data at any time through your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about privacy or cookies? Email support@nellieconnect.com.
