Cookies

Last updated: 17 June 2026

We keep cookies and similar storage to the minimum needed to run the site and, only with your consent, to understand how it is used. We do not use advertising cookies, and we never sell your information or share it with advertisers.

What we use, and why

Essential local storage

When you start a purchase, your browser stores a small amount of information so the checkout works. This includes a one-off reference (a "device token") and the details you enter, kept on your device so your account can be created after payment, for example if you return to the page. This stays in your browser, is not used to track you, and is not shared.

Payment (Stripe)

When you pay, our payment provider Stripe sets its own essential cookies to process the payment securely and help prevent fraud. You can read more in Stripe's cookie policy.

Analytics (PostHog), only if you accept

If you accept on our cookie banner, we use PostHog, hosted in the European Union, to understand how people move through the site so we can keep making it gentler and easier. This sets a small number of cookies on your device. We load it only after you accept, we use it to improve the site and never for advertising, and you can change your mind at any time.

What we don't use

We do not use advertising cookies, and we do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers. Analytics cookies are only ever set if you accept them, and never to market to you elsewhere.

Managing cookies

You can accept or decline analytics using our cookie banner, and reopen your choice any time with the link above. You can also control or clear cookies and site data through your browser settings. Blocking essential items may stop the checkout from working properly, but you can browse the rest of the site normally.
